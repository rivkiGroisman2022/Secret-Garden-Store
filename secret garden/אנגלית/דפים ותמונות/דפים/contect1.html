Activity in the days of Corona


Is the parking open? Is it possible to collect orders from the branch?


The parking lots are open for self-collection. It is recommended to be updated during business hours on the branches page You can also get most of the products delivered to your home at a cost of 79 NIS.

I ordered a self-collection product when can it be collected?


Can be picked up independently from the branch within 7 business days, the branch will send a message immediately that the order is ready for collection Branches can be reached on Sundays-Thursdays until 18:00 or on Fridays between 09:00 and 12:00.

At this time it is not possible to pay on the site with the help of characters
